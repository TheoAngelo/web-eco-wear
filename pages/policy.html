Our Policy

Sustainability

We’re committed to low-impact fashion from start to finish. That means:

  • Organic & recycled materials only
  • Plastic-free packaging
  • Low-waste production practices
  • Carbon-neutral shipping on all orders

    • Ethical Manufacturing

    All Ecowear products are made in fair-wage, ethical factories. No child labor, no sweatshops, no shady stuff—ever. We partner with certified manufacturers who value people as much as we do the planet.

    Returns & Exchanges

    We’re committed to low-impact fashion from start to finish. That means:

  • 30-day return window for unused, unwashed items
  • Free exchanges
  • Final sale items are not eligible for return
  • Email us at support@ecowear.com to get started

    • Our Vow

    Ecowear stands for transparency, progress, and people-powered change. We’re not perfect—but we’re always evolving. If something’s off, tell us—we’re here to make it right.
